This thread is in active development with our partners at the
University of Edinburgh and will be expanded here as the work matures.

The cross-institutional partnership has been the foundation of every
substantive piece of modernization work on the Session Papers since
2017. The current generation of joint work between the UVA Law Library
and the **Centre for Research Collections (CRC) at the University of
Edinburgh Main Library** is unfolding through ongoing conversations
between the two institutions; the specifics will be described on this
page once those conversations have produced arrangements that are
appropriate to share publicly.

In the meantime, the partnership context for the project is summarized
on the [**About**](/scos-modernization-site/about/) page, and the
broader landscape of holding institutions is described under
[**Where the Papers Live**](/scos-modernization-site/collection/holdings/).
